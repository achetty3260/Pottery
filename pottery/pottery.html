ALL ABOUT POTTERY!

In this webpage, we will talk about the art of pottery. This page will inform you the basics of pottery and show examples of ancient pottery.

What is Pottery?

Pottery is works of art created using clay. Pottery can broadly be divided into earthware, porcelain, and stonewear.

Materials:

  • Clay
  • Sponge and water
  • Scoring tools
  • Wire clay cutters
  • Cutting surface

Steps:

  1. Using wire cutters create a block of clay the size of your palm
  2. Knead the clay until soft
  3. Spritz your hands with water and begin molding the clay
  4. On your cutting surface create ornaments for your pot
  5. Using the scoring tools -scrape the pot and fasten the details on
  6. Smooth out the edges
  7. Add a glaze or put your work straight into the kiln
  8. Done
